New Operation Requirements
This page is intended to serve as a checklist for things to request from the Operators when our help is requested on a new operation.
Ask These Questions

What type of access do they have?
Where did they get the config? (from the actual device or a management server)
How did they get the config? ('show run', SNMPSimple Network Management Protocol walk)
How old is the config?
What modules are present on the device?
Is telnet enabled?
Is SNMPSimple Network Management Protocol enabled?  Do we have the community string?
Is SSHSecure Shell enabled?
